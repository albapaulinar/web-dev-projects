Welcome!


Welcome to TechSavvy! If you are a student, faculty or staff from Smith School of Business at the University of Maryland, then this site is for you. We are a company that installs A.V. system in classrooms and conference rooms with the latest technology.


Smith School of Business classroom
with A.V. technology

We want you to feel comfortable using technology to help you accomplish your goals. Each classroom and conference room is equipped with A.V. system. This includes a touch panel, a desktop computer, projectors, VGA cables, HDMI cables, thunderbolt adapters and document cameras. This website will walk you through every step to learn how you can control all this equipment to teach, learn, explore and share.


If you have further questions, do not hesitate to contact us. You can do so either by phone at 301-123-4567, via email smithav@techsavvy.net or by filling out this form.
